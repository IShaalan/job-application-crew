# Positioning Strategy — CloudSync Senior PM, Data Platform

## Fit Score: 8/10

**Why 8 and not higher:** Alex has strong platform PM experience and a technical background, but lacks direct data infrastructure/pipeline experience. The DataFlow analytics role is the closest match. The engineering background at CodeBase (APIs, event systems) bridges the gap credibly.

---

## What is the Hiring Manager Actually Looking For?

CloudSync processes 500M+ events/day. They need someone who:

1. **Understands data infrastructure** — not just consumer features, but pipelines, latency, reliability.
2. **Can run discovery at enterprise scale** — 1,200+ customers means segmentation matters.
3. **Has shipped 0-to-1 platform products** — not just feature iterations on existing products.
4. **Drives cross-functional alignment** — engineering, design, sales, CS all named in the JD.
5. **Is data-driven** — SQL, analytics tools, experimentation called out explicitly.

The HM would say "this person gets it" if they see: platform thinking, technical credibility, customer discovery rigor, and metrics-driven decision-making.
