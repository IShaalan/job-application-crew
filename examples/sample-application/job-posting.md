# Senior Product Manager — CloudSync

**Company:** CloudSync
**Location:** Seattle, WA (Hybrid)
**Team:** Data Platform

## About CloudSync

CloudSync is a B2B SaaS company helping mid-market and enterprise teams unify their data across cloud providers. Our platform processes 500M+ events daily and serves 1,200+ customers globally.

## About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Product Manager to own the Data Platform product line. You will define the roadmap for our real-time data ingestion and transformation layer, working closely with engineering, design, and customer success teams. This role reports to the VP of Product.

## Responsibilities

- Own the end-to-end product lifecycle for the data platform, from discovery through launch and iteration.
- Define product strategy and roadmap aligned to company OKRs and customer needs.
- Partner with engineering leads to make build/buy/integrate decisions for data infrastructure.
- Run discovery programs including customer interviews, competitive analysis, and data-driven prioritization.
- Drive cross-functional alignment across engineering, design, sales, and customer success.
- Define and track success metrics (adoption, latency, reliability, NPS).

## Requirements

- 5+ years of product management experience, ideally in B2B SaaS or data/infrastructure products.
- Experience with data pipelines, APIs, or platform products.
- Strong analytical skills — comfortable with SQL and product analytics tools.
- Proven track record of shipping products from 0 to 1 and scaling adoption.
- Excellent communication skills for technical and non-technical audiences.
- Experience with agile development methodologies.

## Nice to Have

- Technical background (CS degree or engineering experience).
- Experience with experimentation frameworks and A/B testing.
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, Azure).
